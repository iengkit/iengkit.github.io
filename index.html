Wanderlust - Social Networking App (C#, Microsoft Azure) [In progress, anticipated release date: late April]

The idea is to give those who are traveling in a foreign country the ability to find others that have similar backgrounds, interests and language abilities. Wanderlust allows an online community of travellers to flourish and meet each other more easily while in a foreign land. Cultural Compass Web Application (Java, Object-oriented Design, UI Design, Facebook API, GWT)

Do you ever find yourself bored on the weekend with nothing to do? Don’t know what interesting places there are to explore nearby? The Culture Compass is here to solve all these problems! This web application will create a list of potential Cultural Landmarks in Vancouver to explore! These Cultural Places are all within Vancouver and the application will also offer the fastest route for the user to get there, via Google Maps API. If you aren’t sure where you want to go, the Cultural Compass will lead you to make an informed decision by presenting relevant information about the places you plan to go to such as the address or keyword search feature. If you know the name of the place you want to be at but don’t know how to get there, simply do a Keyword search by the name of the place and the Culture Compass will present a route for you to be there! As well, save places while you search around to your profile so you can access them the next time you log in and you will never be bored ever again!
